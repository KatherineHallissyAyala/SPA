Why incorporate technology into a K-12 curriculum?

Inspire Critical Thinking

When teachers incorporate technology into the school day, there is usually a project involved. Projects allow kids to make decisions, require them to present their knowledge in their own way, and necessitate critical thinking.

Meet Kids Where They Are

Kids tend to be fluent users of technology. Planning to have them use technology during the school day is like letting them speak their native language at school.

Prepare for Tomorrow's Challenges

If we want to build a workforce that is ready to lead in science, technology, math, and engineering, we need to build those skills from a young age.
